Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for paid events?
Use the registration form on this page. After selecting a paid event, the fee will be displayed. Payment instructions will be sent to your email after submission.
Can I cancel my registration?
Yes, you can cancel up to 48 hours before the event starts by contacting our support team through the Contact section.
Are the events family-friendly?
Most of our community events are family-friendly. Specific age restrictions, if any, will be mentioned in the event details popup.
